Why Make Pasta?

Pasta is amazing - we all know that. Making pasta from scratch is the only way to make it. You get the authentic feel of making it yourself, and then understanding the value of tasty pasta by having full control over your own ingredients.

Ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour
½ teaspoon salt
1 egg, beaten
2 tablespoons water (Optional)
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 teaspoon packed brown sugar
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
2 teaspoons dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 bay leaf
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Steps for Pasta

1. First, make a nest with the flour on a clean work surface.
2. Next, use your hands to gently mix in the flour.
3. Then, knead!
4. When the dough comes together, shape it into a ball and wrap it in plastic wrap
5. After the dough rests, slice it into 4 pieces.
6. Use a rolling pin or your hands to gently flatten one into an oval disk.
7. Then, run it through the widest setting of your pasta maker.
8. Next, fold the dough… if you want to.
9. Then, fold it in half lengthwise to form a rectangle.
10 .Once you’ve folded the dough, roll it out to your desired thickness.
11. Repeat these steps with the remaining dough pieces.
12. Finally, cut and cook the pasta!
